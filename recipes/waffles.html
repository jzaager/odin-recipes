Easy Weekend Waffles

A beautiful stack of fruit covered waffles

A perfect recipe for Sunday morning with the family.

The fluffiest waffles you'll ever make!

Goes great topped with fruit & syrup. Add eggs and bacon/sausage for a feast!

Ingredients

  • 6 tablespoons unsalted butter
  • 2 cups/240 grams all-purpose flour
  • 1 tablespoon/15 grams sugar
  • 1 teaspoon/8 grams baking powder
  • 1 teaspoon/5 grams fine sea salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon/3 grams baking soda
  • 1 cup plain yogurt
  • 1 cup milk
  • 4 large eggs
